ABOUT

The AA offers a large range of services that cater for every motoring and travelling need.
Since pioneering roadside assistance in South Africa, we have gone on to add a range of exclusive Member-only benefits and savings. From roadworthiness testing and free up to date road information to maps, luggage and air tickets, we provide solutions you can trust, giving you everything you need to get you moving, keep you moving and move you forward.
Our Mission
To be the trusted mobility solutions partner - providing AA service and meeting customers' needs with passion and pride.
Our Vision
The AA of South Africa seeks to be the champion of the South African Motorist and Traveller.
Why should I become an AA Member?
The AA offers a wide range of services to cater for all your motoring and travel needs. Our experienced technicians fix 95% of cars on the spot, allowing motorists to continue on their journey. Members also gain access to discounted rates on our range of valuable services including the technical services offered by our partner DEKRA, driver training and motor-related technical advice, as well as a comprehensive set of rewards from our loyalty programme, Mobility.
